Guitar Kambi Mele Nindru

Guitar Kambi Mele Nindru is part of the Netflix anthology Navarasa (2021), which is a collection of short films based on the nine emotions of the human experience, known as navarasas. Directed by Gautham Menon, this segment features actor Suriya in a role where he plays a musician. The story revolves around his character's emotional journey, with the backdrop of his music serving as a core element. The film includes a romantic angle where Suriya's character is deeply connected to a female lead played by Prayaga Martin.

The music of the film is a major highlight, with a significant focus on melodies that capture the essence of the emotions explored in the plot.
