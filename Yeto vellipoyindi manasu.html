Yeto Vellipoindi Manasu

Yeto Vellipoyindhi Manasu (2012) is a heartfelt romantic drama directed by Gautham Menon, showcasing his mastery in exploring the intricacies of relationships. The film is the Telugu version of his bilingual project, with Nani and Samantha Ruth Prabhu in the lead roles. Unlike typical love stories, this movie delves into the evolution of love across different stages of life, capturing the emotional highs and lows that come with it.

Gautham Menon adopts a subtle and realistic approach to storytelling, making the characters and their emotions feel relatable and authentic. The performances by Nani and Samantha are exemplary, with their chemistry and emotional depth breathing life into the narrative. Samantha, in particular, received widespread acclaim for her portrayal of Nithya, a character full of grace and complexity.
